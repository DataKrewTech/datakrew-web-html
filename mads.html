WHAT IS MADS

MADS is a transformative & secure Open source OS for connected IoT to empower Intelligent Enterprise

Today, the key to innovation and success in the IoT space lies in the ability to experiment quickly, adapt to market needs, and deploy solutions quickly.

MADS believes in removing the complexities of IoT and accelerates the time to value of IoT deployments by providing integrated, secure, tailor-made apps that enable customers and partners to rapidly design & deploy flexible IoT solutions and extract real-time data from them to drive competitive advantage.
